# PRODUCT.md — Ibn Al-Azhar Docs

## Product name

Ibn Al-Azhar Docs — ابن الأزهر دوكس

## Register

Product surface. Design serves the task.

This is not a flashy marketing site first. It is a practical Arabic-first document processing workspace for Azhar students, teachers, and educational teams.

## Primary users

- Azhar secondary students who need clean study documents.
- Teachers and educational content creators who prepare PDFs, summaries, worksheets, and structured learning materials.
- Small educational teams that need a free-first workflow for converting, organizing, and exporting documents.

## Product purpose

Help users transform educational files into clean, organized, reusable study documents with a calm, trustworthy, Arabic-first experience.

## Brand promise

محتوى تعليمي يليق بتفوق طالب الثانوية الأزهرية.

## Tagline

في بيت كل طالب أزهري.

## Personality

- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Scholarly
- Practical
- Warm without being childish

## Voice

Arabic-first, clear, respectful, concise, and supportive.

Use simple Arabic microcopy that feels native to Egyptian/Azhar students without becoming slang-heavy.

## Anti-references

Avoid:
- Generic purple/blue SaaS gradients.
- Random glassmorphism.
- Fake dashboard metrics.
- Overly futuristic AI visuals.
- Loud neon colors.
- English-first layouts.
- Decorative Islamic motifs used without purpose.
- Childish school-app visuals.
- Excessive shadows and nested cards.

## Product principles

1. Arabic-first.
2. RTL-first.
3. Study-focused, not entertainment-focused.
4. Clear before clever.
5. Trust before excitement.
6. Accessibility before decoration.
7. Free-first without false promises.
8. Professional enough for teachers, simple enough for students.

## Accessibility needs

- Strong text contrast.
- Large readable Arabic typography.
- Clear focus states.
- Keyboard-friendly navigation.
- Mobile-first responsive behavior.
- Reduced-motion-friendly animation.
- Clear loading, empty, error, and success states.
